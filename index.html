CT STEM Kids Club Inc. aka CT STEM KIDS Club is a non-profit organization supporting youth development.


CT STEM Kids Club focuses in the fields of Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics.


The purpose of the organization is to:
  • Provide a great learning environment and treat each other with respect and dignity
  • Help kids in preparing for educational contests.
  • Promote excellence by organizing educational contests.
  • Promote volunteer spirit among kids to serve the community.
  • Help communities from donations.

Our Motto: Boost Confidence!

CT STEM Kids Club provides enrichment programs which boost confidence in children to succeed in the field of Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics.

CT STEM Kids Club Inc. is a Non-Profit Organization supporting youth development. This organization is exempt from federal income tax under Internal Revenue Code (IRC) Section 501 (c)(3). Donors can deduct contributions they make to this organization under IRC section 170.
