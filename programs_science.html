There are many science courses we offer:
Science-Level 1 Science-Level 2 Science-Chemistry Science-Biology
A course where students from various grades in elementary school learn many forms of science. We cover weather, speed, motion and force as well as some topics in math. We focus less on traditional classroom methods and more on expirements. The classes will also talk about a new scientists through the form of "Scientist of the Day" where each class they will learn a new scientists A course following up on level 1. In this course students will continue to learn about scientists using the "Scientist of the day" method. Just like the pervious class, they will be conducting expiriments that will help them understand the topic given in each class. A course where students from middle school learn the key concepts of chemistry. We cover topics such as measurement, matter, the periodic table, and many formulas and laws, such as Boyle's law and Charles' Law. An entirely new course dedicated to middle school biology. We cover concepts such as cells, macromolecules, mitosis/meiosis, ecology, etc.
